Building a Twitter Bot for Designers

Research | Code Prototyping | Implementation

The Goal:

Wanted to make a minimalist tool for generating color palettes. Important that it not fall into the trappings of other Twitter bots. I didn't want to just be vomiting data out into the ether. I wanted users to interact with it.

Our goal was to rebuild her website from the ground up with a focus on reflecting her personality and effectively showcasing her work. Bootstrap is very customizable, so I decided to leverage Bootstrap and write custom styling that extended the framework beyond its default look.

Research:

color Schemer. The perfect art project bot. Wanted to make something more stripped down and usable. It's important that users can copy the color values and paste them into a color editor.

I regularly got feedback from Elissa so that we could iterate on these early sketches and come up with something that made sense.
