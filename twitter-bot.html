Building a Twitter Bot for Designers

Research | Code Prototyping | Implementation

The Goal:

I wanted to make a minimalist tool for generating color palettes. I wanted to ensure that it not fall into the trappings of other Twitter bots. I didn't want to just be vomiting data out into the ether. I wanted users to interact with it.

Research:

After doing some research, I discovered that the twitter bot Color Schemer does something similar. Every hour, it tweets a color palette and assigns creative names to each color in the palette. In my opinion it's the perfect art-project bot. I wanted to make something more stripped down and usable, so my design priorities were different for my bot. It was important to me that the user could copy the color values and paste them into a color editor.
